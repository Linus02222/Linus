How to Play

This is a static version of "No Pay No Gain" for iOS users without JavaScript. The full game involves selecting investments, rolling dice, and managing money across three rounds. Below are the rules and options for each round. In the interactive version, you'd:

  • Select up to a maximum number of investments per round
  • Pay the cost of each investment
  • Roll two dice (2-12)
  • Earn profit if the roll meets or exceeds the dice requirement, plus interest
  • Advance to the next round by reaching the target money
